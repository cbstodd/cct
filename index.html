Consumer Convenience Technologies, LLC.

Unique Features of the Push Button Closure. Established in 1999 to provide new technologies in container closures for the food and beverage industries bringing consumers Convenience and Piece of Mind.







Technologies

Through our on-going technological developments, Consumer Convenience Technologies brings to the consumer an improved experience opening a vacuum sealed container. Our leading edge Technologies feature an actual button that once pushed downward releases the vacuum from the container making the closure simpler to remove. After the individual removes their finger from the button, the button may be reset back to its original location for storage of any remaining product for continued freshness through subsequent uses. The current industry standard safety button or raised circular area does not release the vacuum from the container, it only identifies whether or not the vacuum escaped from the container for consumer protection. This feature is not impacted adding the button to closure.

The push button is adaptable to current closure designs for capping containers of all sizes with vacuum or non-vacuumed, cold or pasteurized processes. Our technologies allow us to incorporate the button without the requirement for any additional material. The technologies can either be integrated into current conversion press lines or as a stand-alone conversion process. The installation and final process causes little or minimal degradation to graphics or lithography. The push button is designed below the outer rim of the closure for stacking of containers.
