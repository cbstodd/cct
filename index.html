Patents

Through our on-going technological developments, Consumer Convenience Technologies brings to the consumer an improved experience opening a vacuum sealed container. These leading edge Technologies feature an actual button that once pushed downward releases the vacuum from the container making the closure simpler to remove. After the individual removes their finger from the button, the button rebounds to its original location and seals the closure for storage of any remaining product ensuring continued product freshness through subsequent uses. The current industry standard safety button or raised circular area does not release the vacuum from the container, it only identifies whether or not the vacuum escaped from the container for consumer protection. This feature is not impacted adding the button to closure.

The push button is adaptable to current closure designs for capping containers of all sizes with vacuum or non-vacuumed, cold or pasteurized processes. These technologies allows us to incorporate the button without the requirement for any additional material. The technologies can either be integrated into current conversion press lines or as a stand-alone conversion process. The installation and final process causes little or minimal degradation to graphics or lithography.
